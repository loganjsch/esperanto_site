## The Scenario…

Verifiable platform trust is no longer optional.

Whether you're a business running your services on someone else's cloud, or an enterprise building a security and compliance baseline across your IoT fleet, you need a way to prove the integrity of your workloads down to the hardware.

Manually attesting even a single platform at scale is complicated. When you have multiple platforms and environments—each with its own security scheme—it becomes an inconsistent, fragmented, and unscalable operational nightmare.

---

## What Ratatouille Does

Ratatouille makes it practical to deploy RATS-based trust across your infrastructure. Provides a system to let you define, distribute, and continuously enforce trusted execution policies across your infrastructure without having to integrate or manage the underlying attestation components or logic yourself.

## See It in Action

Ratatouille is in **development** — while the demo is a proof of concept and demonstration of use, it is **currently** wildly insecure, bloated, and probably very buggy.
